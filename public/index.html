Wilder Beard Company

Purveyors of Men's Grooming Products

Handcrafted by Local Artisans

Our products are proudly created in an area steeped in history and lumberman culture. At Wilder Beard Company, we strive to capture the essence of this history in our fragrances - each tied to our woodsman roots. Even our logo has been inspired by this history as a reminder of the log marks once used by the mills surrounding our lake.
